4NIMALS

  • The first batch of 2500 4nimals will be released. To kickstart our journey, 10% of all royalties made by 4nimals sales will be directly donated to World Wildlife Fund

  • Second batch of 5000 4nimals will be released. From this batch, 20% of all royalties will be directly donated to World Wildlife Fund.

  • With the success of the first starting steps, we will giveaway 10 Ethereum back to everyone who contributed to making this happen! As this success could not have been achieved without the support of this incredible community, we want to give back as a token of our appreciation.

  • From this point on, we will start donating 50% of all royalties made by sales as the previous steps helped us get here.

  • The team will create a liquidity pool for our 4nimals. This will allow us to donate to World Wildlife Fund sustainably for years to come.
